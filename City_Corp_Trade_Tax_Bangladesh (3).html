1.3  City Corporation Model Tax Schedule, 2016

Local Government (City Corporation) Act, 2009 — Section 84  |  Applicable to ALL City Corporations in Bangladesh

1
Short Title & Application
This schedule is called the City Corporation Model Tax Schedule, 2016 and applies to all City Corporations in Bangladesh.
2
Definitions
(a) 'Act' = Local Government (City Corporation) Act, 2009
(b) 'Building' = as defined under Section 2(5) of the Act
(c) 'City Corporation' = any City Corporation formed under the Act
3
Tax on Buildings & Land  Max 7%
(1) Maximum 7% of annual value of buildings and land. Minimum BDT 1.
(2) Religious institutions exempt — commercial portions taxable.
(3) Freedom fighters' residential portion (up to 1,000 sq ft) exempt.
(4) Annual value based on actual use and rent receipts.
(5) Taxpayer may pay up to 5 years in advance.
4
Immovable Property Transfer Tax  Max 2%
Maximum 2% of stated value on each property transfer deed.
Exempt: Donations to registered religious/charitable/sports/cultural organizations, and legal debt repayment transfers.
5
Waste Disposal Rate  Max 7%
Maximum 7% of annual tax assessment of service users' buildings (including proportionate collection costs).
6
Street Lighting Rate  Max 5%
Maximum 5% of annual tax assessment of service users' buildings (including proportionate collection costs).
7
Water Rate  Max 3%
Maximum 3% for water supply maintenance. Not applicable where a Water Supply & Sewerage Authority (Act 1996) already collects water charges.
8
Health Tax  Max 8%
Maximum 8% of annual tax assessment of service users' buildings (including proportionate collection costs).
9
City Tax on Hotel Guests
Tax on room rent from hotel guests in City Corporation areas:
5-Star Hotel5%
4-Star Hotel4%
3-Star Hotel3%
Hotel management collects and remits to the City Corporation.
10
Tax on Business, Trade, Profession or Industry
(1) Business size determined by floor area in square feet.
(2) City Corporations submit size classification proposals to Local Government Division.
(3) Trade license fee payable annually, collectible up to 5 years in advance.
